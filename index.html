"Agroforestry is any sustainable land use system that maintains or increases total yields by combining food crops with tree crops and/or livestock on the same unit of land, either alternately or at the same time, using management practices that suit the social and cultural characterstics of the local people and the economics and ecological conditions of the area. it consists of both ecological and economical intractions between the various components"

The Assam state government gave the Assam Agroforestry Development Board approval to expand the state's agroforestry territory. Assam is the second-largest state in the northeast and higher in terms of green space and agroforestry. According to the premiraly estimates from ICAR-CAFRI reported 0.70 Mha agroforestry area in assam and now we worked out potential agroforestry area for upscaling agroforestry for states.
